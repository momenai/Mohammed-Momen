Foundation Models

Dec 6, 2023, Mohammed Momen

We are witnessing a major revolution in AI as new generative models like Foundation models unlock powerful capabilities, synthesizing content across text, images, video and more. Foundation models can be adapted to downstream tasks like chatting, semantic search, information extraction, image captioning, sentiment analysis, summarization, embedding, translation, and generating new human-like content.

However, these models also bear serious risks - they can perpetuate harmful biases, be misused to spread disinformation, lack transparency, and consolidate power among big tech companies. As Foundation models rapidly advance and transform how we interact with computers and each other, expanding into virtually all sectors, we must act now to ensure this technology develops responsibly and for the benefit of society.

Industry, government, academia and the public should come together to guide the building and deployment of Foundation models in a way that promotes fairness, accountability and shared prosperity.
